# Cultural Adaptation Guidelines

## Arabic Market (Middle East & North Africa)

### Writing Direction
- **RTL (Right-to-Left)**: All content must be RTL-friendly
- Use proper Arabic fonts that support RTL
- Test layout and formatting with Arabic text

### Cultural Sensitivity
- **Religious Considerations**: Avoid content that conflicts with Islamic values
- **Family Values**: Emphasize family benefits and group usage
- **Modesty**: Avoid overly flashy or boastful language
- **Respect**: Use formal and respectful tone

### Trust & Credibility
- **Official Warranties**: Emphasize warranty periods (2+ years preferred)
- **Certifications**: Mention official certifications (CE, ISO, etc.)
- **Local Support**: Highlight local customer service availability
- **Authenticity**: Use "original" and "genuine" indicators

### Preferred Messaging
- Durability and long-term value
- Official guarantees and return policies
- Quality over price (avoid "cheap" positioning)
- Family-oriented benefits

### Regional Variations
- **Gulf Countries (UAE, Saudi, Qatar)**: Premium positioning, luxury emphasis
- **Egypt**: Value-focused, practical benefits
- **North Africa**: French influence, different dialect preferences

## Russian Market

### Communication Style
- **Direct and Technical**: Russians prefer straightforward, detailed information
- **No Fluff**: Avoid marketing fluff and exaggerated claims
- **Evidence-Based**: Support claims with specifications and data

### Trust Factors
- **Durability**: Emphasize build quality and longevity
- **Technical Specs**: Detailed parameters are essential
- **Official Documentation**: Manuals, certifications, compliance
- **Local Presence**: Russian language support, local warehouses

### Seasonal Considerations
- **Harsh Climate**: Products should handle extreme conditions
- **Long Winters**: Emphasize indoor use cases
- **Dacha Culture**: Outdoor/cottage usage scenarios

### Pricing Psychology
- Value for money is important
- Quality justifies higher prices
- Discounts should be meaningful (not gimmicky)

---

## Spanish Market (Latin America)

### Regional Differences

#### Mexico
- **Price Sensitive**: Look for deals and discounts
- **Cash/Credit**: Multiple payment options important
- **Delivery**: Free shipping is a major selling point

#### Argentina
- **Installments**: "Cuotas" (payment plans) are crucial
- **Economic Context**: Value and durability emphasized
- **Local Brands**: Some preference for local or regional brands

#### Colombia/Chile/Peru
- **Quality Focus**: Balance of price and quality
- **US Influence**: Open to US brands and products
- **Growing E-commerce**: Increasing comfort with online purchases

#### Spain
- **Quality Over Price**: Willing to pay for quality
- **Environmental**: Eco-friendly features resonate
- **EU Standards**: CE marking and EU compliance important

### Language Variations
- Use neutral Spanish for broad reach
- Adapt vocabulary for specific countries when targeting
- Avoid regional slang that may not translate

### Cultural Values
- **Family**: Family-oriented messaging works well
- **Social Proof**: Reviews and testimonials important
- **Fiesta Culture**: Products for social gatherings popular

## English Market (US/UK/Global)

### US Market
- **Convenience**: Fast shipping, easy returns
- **Reviews**: Social proof is crucial
- **Deals**: Responsive to sales and promotions
- **Individualism**: Personal benefits emphasized

### UK Market
- **Quality**: British consumers value quality craftsmanship
- **Understatement**: Avoid over-the-top marketing
- **Service**: Customer service expectations are high
- **Sustainability**: Environmental concerns increasingly important

### Australian Market
- **Outdoor Lifestyle**: Products for outdoor activities popular
- **Durability**: Must withstand harsh conditions
- **Directness**: Straightforward communication preferred
- **Local**: Preference for local or regional shipping

### Universal English Guidelines
- Clear, concise messaging
- Strong CTAs
- Mobile-first approach
- Fast loading and easy navigation

## Color & Imagery Considerations

### Arabic Markets
- **Green**: Positive, associated with Islam
- **Gold**: Premium, luxury
- **Avoid**: Colors with negative cultural associations

### Russian Market
- **Red**: Power, energy (positive)
- **Blue**: Trust, reliability
- **Gold/Yellow**: Quality, premium

### Latin America
- **Bright Colors**: Generally well-received
- **Yellow**: Attention, energy
- **Green**: Nature, environmental

### Western Markets
- **Blue**: Trust, professionalism (universal)
- **Green**: Environmental, money, growth
- **Orange**: Energy, calls-to-action
- **Black**: Luxury, sophistication

---

## Payment & Shipping Preferences

### Arabic Markets
- Cash on delivery (COD) still popular
- Local payment gateways (Mada, KNET)
- Express shipping expectations

### Russian Market
- Cash on delivery common
- Local payment systems (YooMoney, etc.)
- Long delivery times accepted if communicated

### Latin America
- Installment payments essential in some countries
- Local payment methods (OXXO, PagoEfectivo)
- Free shipping thresholds

### Western Markets
- Credit cards dominant
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Fast/free shipping expectations
