# SEO Strategies by Search Engine

## Google / Bing (English & Spanish Markets)

### Core Principles
- **E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
- **Semantic Search**: Use related terms and natural language variations
- **User Intent**: Match content to search intent (informational, transactional, navigational)

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Title Tags**
   - Lead with primary keyword
   - Include power words (Best, Top, Ultimate)
   - Keep under 60 characters
   - Example: "Best Solar Camping Lantern 2024 | Waterproof & Portable"

2. **Meta Descriptions**
   - Include primary + secondary keywords
   - Add clear CTA
   - Keep under 155 characters
   - Example: "Shop the best solar camping lantern with 5000mAh battery. IP65 waterproof, foldable design. Free shipping & 30-day returns!"

3. **Content Structure**
   - Use H2/H3 tags with semantic keywords
   - Include FAQ section for featured snippets
   - Add schema markup for products
   - Optimize for rich snippets (ratings, price, availability)

4. **Keyword Strategy**
   - Focus on long-tail keywords with commercial intent
   - Use LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords
   - Include question-based keywords (How to, What is, Best way to)

## Google (French Market)

### Core Principles
- **Quality Certification**: French consumers value official certifications (CE, NF, etc.)
- **Eco-Friendly**: Environmental consciousness is high; emphasize energy efficiency
- **Design Aesthetics**: French consumers appreciate elegant design and style

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Title Tags**
   - Include quality indicators ("Qualité", "Premium")
   - Mention eco-friendly features when applicable
   - Example: "Meilleur Purificateur d'Air 2024 | Qualité Premium & Écoénergétique"

2. **Meta Descriptions**
   - Highlight certifications and guarantees
   - Mention French customer service if available
   - Example: "Achetez le meilleur purificateur d'air. Qualité garantie CE. Livraison gratuite en France."

3. **Content Structure**
   - Detailed product specifications
   - Environmental impact information
   - Design and aesthetic appeal

4. **Keyword Strategy**
   - "meilleur [produit]"
   - "[produit] qualité"
   - "[produit] écologique"
   - "[produit] pas cher" (value-conscious)

## Google / Yahoo Japan (Japanese Market)

### Core Principles
- **Compact Design**: Space efficiency is crucial in Japan
- **Politeness**: Use polite, respectful language (です/ます調)
- **Detail-Oriented**: Japanese consumers expect thorough product information
- **Energy Efficiency**: Important for eco-conscious Japanese consumers

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Title Tags**
   - Include size/dimensions (compact is better)
   - Mention energy-saving features

2. **Meta Descriptions**
   - Polite, detailed descriptions

3. **Content Structure**

   - Energy consumption details

4. **Keyword Strategy**

   - "[製品] 人気"
   - "[製品] 口コミ"
   - "[製品] コンパクト"

## Yandex (Russian Market)

### Core Principles
- **Technical Depth**: Detailed specifications matter more than emotional appeal
- **Regional Optimization**: Consider Russian language nuances and regional variations
- **Behavioral Factors**: CTR and dwell time heavily influence rankings

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Title Tags**
   - Include key specifications
   - Use model numbers when applicable
   - Example: "Кемпинговый фонарь солнечный 5000mAh IP65 - купить с доставкой"

2. **Content Structure**
   - Detailed parameter tables are essential
   - Include comparison tables with competitors
   - Technical specifications should be prominent
   - Use bullet points for features

3. **Keyword Strategy**
   - Focus on transactional keywords with "купить" (buy), "цена" (price)
   - Include specification-based searches
   - Use regional variations (Russian vs. Ukrainian vs. Kazakh)

4. **Cultural Considerations**
   - Emphasize durability and reliability
   - Official warranties are highly valued
   - Local payment methods and delivery options

## Middle East Markets (Arabic)

### Core Principles
- **RTL Optimization**: Right-to-left text direction
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect local customs and values
- **Trust Signals**: Official certifications and warranties crucial

### Optimization Tactics
1. **Title Tags**
   - Lead with quality indicators (أفضل - best, الأصلي - original)
   - Include warranty information when possible
   - Keep under 60 characters (Arabic characters may display differently)
   - Example: "أفضل فانوس شمسي للتخييم 2024 | ضمان سنتين"

2. **Content Structure**
   - RTL-friendly formatting
   - Clear warranty and guarantee sections
   - Official certification badges
   - Local customer service information

3. **Keyword Strategy**
   - Use formal Arabic (Modern Standard Arabic) for broader reach
   - Include dialect variations for specific countries
   - Religious and cultural sensitivity in word choice

4. **Cultural Considerations**
   - Emphasize durability and long-term value
   - Family-oriented benefits resonate well
   - Official guarantees and return policies are essential
   - Local customer support availability

## Universal Best Practices

### Technical SEO
- Mobile-first optimization
- Fast page load speeds (< 3 seconds)
- HTTPS security
- Clean URL structure
- Image optimization with alt tags

### Content Quality
- Original, valuable content
- Regular updates
- Proper grammar and spelling
- Clear, scannable formatting
- Internal linking structure

### Conversion Optimization
- Clear CTAs above the fold
- Trust signals (reviews, certifications)
- Urgency elements (limited stock, sale ending)
- Easy navigation and search
- Multiple payment options
